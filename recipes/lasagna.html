Ingredients

  1. Preheat oven to 2.
  2. Combine slop, ick and ooze in a medium-sized bowl. Stir well.
  3. In a large bowl, combine grime, greasy rag and oregano.
  4. Combine bowl with other bowl. Shake, but do not stir.
  5. Let sit in summer heat for three days.
  6. Throw it in the trash.
